How These Essays Are Made

Every essay starts with an observation of my own, usually from the work. Then I use AI as a sparring partner:

→ The thesis gets attacked first: Is it true? Is it new, or old wine? What survives the pushback gets developed.
→ Parallel research agents gather evidence. What cannot be verified gets cut; every number in the text links to its source.
→ The writing happens in dialogue, over many rounds: I shorten, simplify, and discard terms until the text sounds spoken.
→ At the end, the machine checks the text against my own writing rules, and I decide what stays.

Every thesis, every sharp edge, and every mistake is my decision. The machine delivers the analysis. The commitment is mine.
